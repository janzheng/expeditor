### practical-examples
Search for real-world implementations, case studies, and examples of [topic] in practice. Look for GitHub repos, blog posts, and conference talks. Write a summary of approaches with links.

### contrarian-view
Search for criticisms, limitations, and counterarguments about [topic]. What are the known failure modes? When does it NOT work? Write a balanced assessment of risks and downsides.

## synthesize
